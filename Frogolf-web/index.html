Frogolf is a roguelike deckbuilding about SUICIDAL FROGS PLAYING GOLF!

We have these:

  • Collect clubs
  • Drink tee at 5oklok (tee-gol)
  • Drink biiir at FROGOLF BAR (bar-golf)
  • Hit other frogolfers (true frogolf)
  • Deckbuilding!! 0_0 and trading (trade-golf)

Physics based!

Select a club and bash into enemies (or throw your club into them!)

Create unique builds!

Manage your inventory to find unique combination to fight enemies (yes, you may play only with rocks if you want)

Be prepared

Take neuroleptic-drugs or kill yourself if you lose!
